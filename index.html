ABOUT

Porter Robinson is a producer and DJ from North Carolina. Since 2005, he's created a portfolio of unique and groundbreaking electronic music. His first studio album, Worlds (2014), peaked at #1 on Billboard's Top Dance/Electronic Albums.

Since then, Robinson has made the rounds worldwide. In 2017, he and long-time friend/fellow producer Madeon released their single Shelter which, along with its music video, took the Internet by storm.

Known for experimenting with music, Robinson created Virtual Self in 2017, a hard trance/eurodance-inspired project that hearkened back to music of the late 90s and early 2000s.

Robinson's second studio album, Nurture, is set to release in 2020. Two singles from the album, Get Your Wish and Something Comforting, have given fans a hint as to his new musical and visual direction.
